A new chapter

Hoi, iedereen! Hoe gaat het?

Imagine that your life was written down in a book. Where would you be right now? Are you in the prologue or are you 50 chapters in? For me, I am about to embark on a new and exciting chapter, and I feel as though I'm only a few chapters into my life. This is the part of the book where there is a dramatic scenery change. I'm moving to a new country.



Specifically, the Netherlands. Canals, a lack of hills and an abundance of cheese. En natuurlijk ook een heleboel Nederlanders!
